soyuz missions to the iss were all planned to be only taxi mission to deliver a new soyuz spacecraft as the station's lifeboat every six month with a visiting crew but not for crew exchange until the space shuttle columbia disaster the same was planned for soyuz tma-2 a visiting crew consisting of commander gennady padalka and esa-astronaut pedro duque were to spend about one week at the station and then return with the previous soyuz tma-1 spacecraft the third seat might have gone to the chilean klaus von storch as a chilean space agency (agencia chilena del espacio) cosmonaut but even
