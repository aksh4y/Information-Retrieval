five crew took charge of iss operations on 7 june 2002 an official ceremony between expedition crews took place 10 june with the ceremonial ringing of the station's brass bell symbolizing the transfer of command the expedition five crew carried out approximately 25 new investigations on board the iss as well as continued with various science investigations begun before their stay the crew wrapped
