super hurricane5 mitsubishi versions6 referencesdesign{edit]the hurricane was based on the earlier go-devil flathead engine to get more power from the engine the induction system was changed from the go-devil's side-valve configuration to an inlet-over-exhaust configuration or "f-head" this allowed the valves to be larger and the combustion chamber to be smaller improving flow and increasing the compression ratio{2] the compression ratio rose from 65:1 in the go-devil engine to 75:1 in the hurricane engine although a version of the hurricane engine was made with a 69:1 compression
