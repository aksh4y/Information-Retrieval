festival also just hurricane is a music festival that takes place in scheexc3x9fel near bremen germany usually every june the festival and its guests are generally associated with the alternative part of mainstream music although the festival has been becoming more and more mainstream over the yearsthe southside festival often referred to as the "sister" of hurricane takes place on the same three days in the very south of germany (contrary to hurricane which is in the north) together they are a twin festival since they are on the same days and usually have the same lineup with only few exceptionscontents1 history2 past lineups3 see also4 references5 external linkshistory{edit]in 1973 the first festival was held in scheexc3x9fel the place where today's hurricane takes place it was called "es rockt in der heide" at that time (literally translated: it's rocking in the heath) and was attended by 52000 peopleat the "first rider open air" in 1977 fans caused damage of more than a million marks when riots broke out because only five of the announced 23 bands could performxe2x80x94a huge scandal was uncovered few days later forcing the organizing management to discontinue their festivals in scheexc3x9felin 1997 rock returned to scheexc3x9fel in the form of the hurricane festival with numerous bands performing in front of 20000 visitors it has taken place on the eichenring a motorcycle sand racetrack ever since audience size peaked in 2008 with about 75000 guests; from 2002 to 2006 between 40000 and 60000 people came in 2003 the festival was extended from two to three days in 2007 60000 and in 2008 70000 tickets were sold{1]in 2004 david bowie suffered from chest pain he was flown to the ak altona by helicopter after finishing the show there he was diagnosed with a
