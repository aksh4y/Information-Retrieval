This expedition began in November 2009 when the <a href="/wiki/Expedition_21" title="Expedition 21">Expedition 21</a> crew departed. For a period of 3 weeks, there were only 2 crew members; it was the first time that had happened since <a href="/wiki/STS-114" title="STS-114">STS-114</a>. Commander <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jeff_Williams_(astronaut)" title="Jeff Williams (astronaut)">Jeff Williams</a> and flight engineer <a href="/wiki/Maksim_Surayev" title="Maksim Surayev">Maksim Surayev</a> were joined by the rest of their crew on 22 December 2009, making the Expedition 22 a crew of five.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>\n<p>The expedition ended when <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_TMA-16" title="Soyuz TMA-16">Soyuz TMA-16</a> undocked on 17 March 2010, and was immediately followed by the
