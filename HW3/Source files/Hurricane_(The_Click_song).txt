Click">The Click</a>\'s second album, <i><a href="/wiki/Game_Related" title="Game Related">Game Related</a></i>.</p>\n<p>Produced by Studio Ton, the song became The Click\'s most successful single, peaking at number 63 on the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_100" title="Billboard Hot 100"><i>Billboard</i> Hot 100</a> and number four on the <i>Billboard</i> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rap_Songs" title="Rap Songs">Hot Rap Singles</a>. The song\'s title comes from the <a href="/wiki/Hurricane_(cocktail)" title="Hurricane (cocktail)">alcoholic drink of the same name</a>. The background vocals feature a little know artist named Sapphire from Cleveland, Ohio.
