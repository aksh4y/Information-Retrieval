The same day, Commander Scott Kelly and Timothy Kopra performed a contingency <a href="/wiki/Extravehicular_activity" title="Extravehicular activity">EVA</a> and successfully repaired the <a href="/wiki/Mobile_Servicing_System#Mobile_Base_System" title="Mobile Servicing System">Mobile Base System</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> On 23 December, the Progress MS-1 resupply vehicle docked at the ISS.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>\n<p>On 8 January 2016, pupils from <a href="/wiki/Sandringham_School" title="Sandringham School">Sandringham School</a> in <a href="/wiki/St_Albans" title="St Albans">St Albans</a> made the first <a href="/wiki/Amateur_radio" title="Amateur radio">amateur radio</a> call to a British astronaut at the International Space Station, contacting <a href="/wiki/Tim_Peake" title="Tim Peake">Tim Peake</a> as part of his <i>Principia</i> mission.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>\n<p>On 15 January 2016, Tim Peake and Timothy Kopra began an EVA at 12:55 GMT. A failed voltage regulator (1B <a href="/wiki/Electrical_system_of_the_International_Space_Station#SSU" title="Electrical system of the International Space Station">SSU</a>) was replaced during the EVA.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> This EVA marked the first official spacewalk by a Briton, Tim Peake, who was wearing the <a href="/wiki/Union_Jack" title="Union Jack">Union Jack</a> on his <a href="/wiki/Extravehicular_Mobility_Unit" title="Extravehicular Mobility Unit">EMU</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> NASA decided to finish the spacewalk around 17:00 GMT, as Timothy Kopra reported water in his helmet. The spacesuit, which Kopra was wearing, was the same one worn by <a href="/wiki/Luca_Parmitano" title="Luca Parmitano">Luca Parmitano</a> on 16 July 2013, when his helmet flooded during EVA-23 of <a href="/wiki/Expedition_36" title="Expedition 36">Expedition 36</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>\n<p>On 23 February 2016, Scott Kelly displayed his sense of humour by wearing a <a href="/wiki/Gorilla_suit" title="Gorilla suit">gorilla suit</a>, while chasing Tim Peake through the space station, in a recorded video with the backing track of <i><a href="/wiki/Yakety_Sax" title="Yakety Sax">Yakety Sax</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> The following day, a video message from Tim Peake was played at the <a href="/wiki/2016_Brit_Awards" title="2016 Brit Awards">2016 Brit Awards</a>, giving congratulations to <a href="/wiki/Adele" title="Adele">Adele</a> for her award.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>\n<p>A further EVA was also scheduled to take place during Expedition 46.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="References">References</span><span
