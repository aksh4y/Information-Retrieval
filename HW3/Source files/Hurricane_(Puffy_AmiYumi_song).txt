It is a cover of a song originally performed in 1981 by "Chanels" (later <a href="/wiki/Rats_%26_Star" title="Rats &amp; Star">Rats &amp; Star</a>). The song contains <a href="/wiki/MIDI" title="MIDI">MIDI</a> instruments and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Brass_instruments" title="Brass instruments">brass instruments</a>. The subway version is featured in the album <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Music_from_the_Subway&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Music from the Subway (page does not exist)">Music from
