Station">International Space Station</a> (ISS). The first two crew members, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sergey_Volkov_(astronaut)" title="Sergey Volkov (astronaut)">Sergey Volkov</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Oleg_Kononenko" title="Oleg Kononenko">Oleg Kononenko</a> were launched on 8 April 2008, aboard the <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_TMA-12" title="Soyuz TMA-12">Soyuz TMA-12</a>. Once aboard the station, they joined <a href="/wiki/Garrett_Reisman" title="Garrett Reisman">Garrett Reisman</a>, who transferred from <a href="/wiki/Expedition_16" title="Expedition 16">Expedition 16</a> to join the Expedition 17 crew.</p>\n<p>Reisman was replaced by <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Chamitoff" title="Gregory Chamitoff">Gregory Chamitoff</a>, who launched aboard the <a href="/wiki/Space_Shuttle_Discovery" title="Space Shuttle Discovery">Space Shuttle <i>Discovery</i></a> mission <a href="/wiki/STS-124" title="STS-124">STS-124</a> on 31 May 2008. Volkov and Kononenko landed safely on 24 October 2008, while Chamitoff remained aboard the station as an <a href="/wiki/Expedition_18" title="Expedition 18">Expedition 18</a> crewmember.</p>\n<p></p>\n<div class="toc" id="toc">\n<div class="toctitle">\n<h2>Contents</h2>\n</div>\n<ul>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-1"><a href="#Crew"><span class="tocnumber">1</span>
