href="/wiki/Crooked_Creek_Township,_Bollinger_County,_Missouri" title="Crooked Creek Township, Bollinger County, Missouri">Crooked Creek Township</a> in <a href="/wiki/Bollinger_County,_Missouri" title="Bollinger County, Missouri">Bollinger County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Missouri" title="Missouri">Missouri</a>. The community is located near Hurricane Creek, a nearby stream. Hurricane was named after Hurricane Creek. The name derives from the creek\xe2\x80\x99s unusual swiftness and violence after heavy rains, which makes passage across the creek impossible or dangerous. The violence of this swiftness and speed is likened to a storm or hurricane.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> A post office was in operation between the years 1894-1942.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="References">References</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hurricane,_Bollinger_County,_Missouri&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: References">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<div class="reflist" style="list-style-type: decimal;">\n<div class="mw-references-wrap">\n<ol class="references">\n<li id="cite_note-1"><span class="mw-cite-backlink"><b><a href="#cite_ref-1">^</a></b></span> <span class="reference-text">State Historical Society of Missouri: Bollinger County Place Names, 1928-1945 <a class="external free" href="http://shs.umsystem.edu/manuscripts/ramsay/ramsay_bollinger.html" rel="nofollow">http://shs.umsystem.edu/manuscripts/ramsay/ramsay_bollinger.html</a></span></li>\n<li id="cite_note-2"><span class="mw-cite-backlink"><b><a href="#cite_ref-2">^</a></b></span> <span class="reference-text">Post Offices in Bollinger County, MO <cite class="citation web"><a class="external text" href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131029191155/http://www.missouripostalhistory.org/bo017ml.htm" rel="nofollow">"Archived copy"</a>.
