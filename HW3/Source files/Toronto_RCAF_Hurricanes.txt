The team was composed of football players who had joined the <a href="/wiki/Royal_Canadian_Air_Force" title="Royal Canadian Air Force">Royal Canadian Air Force</a>. The team won the <a href="/wiki/30th_Grey_Cup" title="30th Grey Cup">30th Grey Cup</a> in 1942.</p>\n<p>Fifteen members of the team went on to operational service with the RCAF in Europe during the war.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Seven of those men were killed in combat: <a href="/wiki/Eddie_Thompson_(Canadian_football)" title="Eddie Thompson (Canadian football)">Eddie Thompson</a>, Lloyd Langley, Ed Poscavage, Eddie Burton, Jack Buckmaster, George Oliphant and Bob Sarvis.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a
