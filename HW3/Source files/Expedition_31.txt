Following a series of delays,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> Dragon launched on 22 May 2012, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Spacecraft_docking_and_berthing_mechanisms" title="Spacecraft docking and berthing mechanisms">berthed</a> successfully with the ISS on 25 May, after conducting a series of orbital test manoeuvres.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-DragonLaunch_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-DragonLaunch-10">[10]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> Dragon carried around 544 kilograms (1,199\xc2\xa0lb) of cargo to the ISS, including food, clothing, a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Laptop_computer" title="Laptop computer">laptop computer</a> and 15 student experiments.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-DragonLaunch_10-1"><a href="#cite_note-DragonLaunch-10">[10]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> After being loaded with 660 kilograms (1,460\xc2\xa0lb) of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Downmass" title="Downmass">downmass</a> cargo, including completed experiments and redundant equipment, it undocked from the station and returned to Earth on 31 May 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Accomplished_14-0"><a href="#cite_note-Accomplished-14">[14]</a></sup> Dragon landed intact in the <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Ocean" title="Pacific Ocean">Pacific Ocean</a> and was successfully recovered, allowing SpaceX to begin <a href="/wiki/SpaceX_Dragon#List_of_COTS.2FCRS_missions" title="SpaceX Dragon">regular cargo flights</a> to the ISS.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup> The first such logistics mission, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/CRS_SpX-1" title="CRS SpX-1">CRS SpX-1</a>, launched successfully in October 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup></p>\n<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Soyuz_TMA-03M_departure">Soyuz TMA-03M departure</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Expedition_31&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Soyuz TMA-03M departure">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>\n<p>Soyuz TMA-03M departed from the ISS on 1 July 2012, successfully returning Kononenko, Kuipers and Pettit to Earth. Their departure marked the formal end of Expedition 31, and the beginning of <a href="/wiki/Expedition_32" title="Expedition 32">Expedition 32</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Pete_2-2"><a
