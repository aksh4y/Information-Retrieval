They played at the <a href="/wiki/Waterloo_Memorial_Arena" title="Waterloo Memorial Arena">Waterloo Memorial Arena</a> in <a href="/wiki/Waterloo,_Ontario" title="Waterloo, Ontario">Waterloo, Ontario</a>. The team folded after the 1951\xe2\x80\x9352 season due to financial woes. That season, the Hurricanes competed against cross-town rivals, the <a href="/wiki/Kitchener_Greenshirts" title="Kitchener Greenshirts">Kitchener Greenshirts</a>.</p>\n<p>Two alumni of the Hurricanes went on to play in the <a href="/wiki/National_Hockey_League" title="National Hockey League">National Hockey League</a>. Goaltender <a href="/wiki/Norm_Defelice" title="Norm Defelice">Norm Defelice</a> played 10 games with the <a href="/wiki/Boston_Bruins" title="Boston Bruins">Boston Bruins</a> in <a href="/wiki/1956%E2%80%9357_NHL_season" title="1956\xe2\x80\x9357 NHL season">1956\xe2\x80\x9357</a>, and had a long career in the <a href="/wiki/Eastern_Hockey_League" title="Eastern Hockey League">Eastern Hockey League</a>. Defenceman <a href="/wiki/Warren_Godfrey" title="Warren Godfrey">Warren Godfrey</a> played 15 fifteen seasons in the NHL with the Boston Bruins and <a href="/wiki/Detroit_Red_Wings" title="Detroit Red
