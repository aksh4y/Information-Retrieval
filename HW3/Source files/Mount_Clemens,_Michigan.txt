class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mount_Clemens,_Michigan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>Mount Clemens was first surveyed in 1795 by Christian Clemens, who settled there four years later. Clemens and his friend, John Brooks, built a distillery, which helped settle the area. Brooks and Clemens platted the land, and the town was named after Clemens in 1818. It received a post office in 1821, with <a href="/wiki/John_Stockton_(Michigan)" title="John Stockton (Michigan)">John Stockton</a> as the first postmaster. It filed for incorporation as a village in 1837, but it was not acted upon until 1851. It was later incorporated as a city in 1879. Christian Clemens is buried at Clemens Park, located just north of downtown. It became the seat of Macomb County on March 11, 1818.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-google_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-google-8">[8]</a></sup></p>\n<p>The Mount Clemens Public Library opened in 1865.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>\n<p>Historically, Mount Clemens\' largest industry was the <a href="/wiki/Mineral_spa" title="Mineral spa">mineral baths</a> that were scattered throughout the city from 1873 until 1974. The city once encompassed 11 bathhouses and several hotels at its peak. The first bathhouse was built in 1873 and was known as \xe2\x80\x9cThe Original\xe2\x80\x9d and was located on the corner of Jones and Water Street. The bathhouse remained until 1883 when the building burned, yet was rebuilt in 1884 and accommodated larger crowds. Over the years, noted visitors such as film actors <a href="/wiki/Clark_Gable" title="Clark Gable">Clark Gable</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mae_West" title="Mae West">Mae West</a>, athletes <a href="/wiki/Babe_Ruth" title="Babe Ruth">Babe Ruth</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jack_Dempsey" title="Jack Dempsey">Jack Dempsey</a>, news magnate <a href="/wiki/William_Randolph_Hearst" title="William Randolph Hearst">William Randolph Hearst</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Vanderbilt_family" title="Vanderbilt family">Vanderbilt family</a> vacationed in the city for the bath industry.</p>\n<p>The only remaining bathhouse from the bath era of Mount Clemens is St. Joseph\'s Sanitarium and Bath House, which has been recently renamed Select Specialty Hospital and owned by Select Medical Corporation. This last bath house is in danger of being demolished while the Friends of Historic Preservation <a class="external autonumber" href="http://bathcityusa.com" rel="nofollow">[1]</a> are working with the city to preserve it.</p>\n<p>The Olympia Salon &amp; Spa, located in the Martha Washington Sanitarium on Cass Ave, are rejuvenating the bath era by offering mineral baths once again.</p>\n<p>Throughout the late 20th Century, the suburban expansion of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Metropolitan_Detroit" title="Metropolitan Detroit">Metropolitan Detroit</a> and its <a href="/wiki/Commuter_town" title="Commuter town">exurbs</a> encompassed the city of Mt. Clemens as well as its surrounding townships.</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Art_and_culture">Art and culture</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mount_Clemens,_Michigan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Art and culture">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The <a href="/wiki/Anton_Art_Center" title="Anton Art Center">Anton Art Center</a> is a community gallery offering exhibitions of artwork by local, national and international artists. The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 4,008.4 inhabitants per square mile (1,547.7/km<sup>2</sup>). There were 7,582 housing units at an average density of 1,862.9 per square mile (719.3/km<sup>2</sup>). The racial makeup of the city was 70.0% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">White</a>, 24.8% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/African_American_(U.S._Census)" title="African American (U.S. Census)">African American</a>, 0.3% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Native_American_(U.S._Census)" title="Native American (U.S. Census)">Native American</a>, 0.5% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Asian_(U.S._Census)" title="Asian (U.S. Census)">Asian</a>, 0.8% from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Race_(U.S._Census)" title="Race (U.S. Census)">other races</a>, and 3.6% from two or more races. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hispanic_(U.S._Census)" title="Hispanic (U.S. Census)">Hispanic</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latino_(U.S._Census)" title="Latino (U.S. Census)">Latino</a> of any race were 2.9% of the population.</p>\n<p>There were 6,714 households of which 25.9% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 30.6% were married couples living together, 16.4% had a female householder with no husband present, 5.7% had a male householder with no wife present, and 47.2% were non-families. 39.7% of all households were made up of individuals and 13% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.19 and the average family size was 2.96.</p>\n<p>The median age in the city was 38.3 years. 20.6% of residents were under the age of 18; 9.7% were between the ages of 18 and 24; 28.9% were from 25 to 44; 27.8% were from 45 to 64; and 13% were 65 years of age or older. The gender makeup of the city was 51.5% male and 48.5% female.</p>\n<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2000_census">2000 census</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mount_Clemens,_Michigan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: 2000 census">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>\n<p>As of the census<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR2_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-GR2-5">[5]</a></sup> of 2000, there were 17,312 people, 7,073 households, and 3,854 families residing in the city. The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 4,107.0 per square mile (1,583.9/km<sup>2</sup>). There were 7,546 housing units at an average density of 1,790.2 per square mile (690.4/km<sup>2</sup>). The racial makeup of the city was 75.79% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">White</a>, 19.61% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/African_American_(U.S._Census)" title="African American (U.S. Census)">African American</a>, 0.73% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Native_American_(U.S._Census)" title="Native American (U.S. Census)">Native American</a>, 0.49% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Asian_(U.S._Census)" title="Asian (U.S. Census)">Asian</a>, 0.02% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pacific_Islander_(U.S._Census)" title="Pacific Islander (U.S. Census)">Pacific Islander</a>, 0.76% from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Race_(United_States_Census)" title="Race (United States Census)">other races</a>, and 2.59% from two or more races. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hispanic_(U.S._Census)" title="Hispanic (U.S. Census)">Hispanic</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latino_(U.S._Census)" title="Latino (U.S. Census)">Latino</a> of any race were 2.33% of the population.</p>\n<p>There were 7,073 households out of which 24.7% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 35.2% were married couples living together, 14.7% had a female householder with no husband present, and 45.5% were non-families. 39.2% of all households were made up of individuals and 13.2% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.21 and the average family size was 2.99.</p>\n<p>In the city, the population was spread out with 21.6% under the age of 18, 9.0% from 18 to 24, 34.3% from 25 to 44, 21.7% from 45 to 64, and 13.4% who were 65 years of age or older. The median age was 36 years. For every 100 females there were 107.1 males. For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 105.8 males.</p>\n<p>The median income for a household in the city was $37,856, and the median income for a family was $50,518. Males had a median income of $41,005 versus $27,896 for females. The <a href="/wiki/Per_capita_income" title="Per capita income">per capita income</a> for the city was $21,741. About 10.0% of families and 14.1% of the population were below the poverty line, including 20.1% of those under age 18 and 11.9% of those age 65 or over.</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Government">Government</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mount_Clemens,_Michigan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Government">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The city government is composed of a mayor, the current being <a href="/wiki/Barb_Dempsey" title="Barb Dempsey">Barb Dempsey</a>, and a city council.
