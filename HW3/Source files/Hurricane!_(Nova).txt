You can purchase it on ShopPBS.org</div>\n</div>\n</div>\n<p><b>Hurricane!</b> (episode: 1616 (308)) is a <i><a href="/wiki/Nova_(TV_series)" title="Nova (TV series)">Nova</a></i> episode that <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/List_of_NOVA_episodes" title="List of NOVA episodes">aired on</a> November 7, 1989 on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Public_Broadcasting_Service" title="Public Broadcasting Service">PBS</a>. The episode describes the fury of a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hurricane" title="Hurricane">hurricane</a> and the history of hurricane forecasting. The episode features footage of <a href="/wiki/Hurricane_Camille" title="Hurricane Camille">Hurricane Camille</a> of 1969 and <a href="/wiki/Hurricane_Gilbert" title="Hurricane Gilbert">Hurricane Gilbert</a> of 1988 and behind the scenes footage at the <a href="/wiki/National_Hurricane_Center" title="National Hurricane Center">National Hurricane Center</a> as forecasters tracked Hurricane Gilbert from its formation to its landfall in northern <a href="/wiki/Mexico" title="Mexico">Mexico</a>. Meteorologists, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Hugh_Willoughby_(meteorologist)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Hugh Willoughby (meteorologist) (page does not exist)">Hugh Willoughby</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bob_Sheets" title="Bob Sheets">Bob Sheets</a> (then director of the <a href="/wiki/National_Hurricane_Center" title="National Hurricane Center">National Hurricane Center</a>) and Jeff Masters were shown in the episode.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-a_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-a-1">[1]</a></sup> The episode was released on <a href="/wiki/VHS" title="VHS">VHS</a> home video under the same episode title and distributed by <a href="/wiki/WGBH-TV" title="WGBH-TV">WGBH</a> Boston video.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> In 2004, the <i>Nova</i> episode was released on <a href="/wiki/DVD" title="DVD">DVD</a> and featured a different cover picture and title style.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> In 1997, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/UAV_Corp." title="UAV Corp.">UAV Corp.</a> also distributed the <i>Nova</i> episode under the title Deadly Hurricanes which contains additional footage of <a href="/wiki/Hurricane_Andrew" title="Hurricane Andrew">Hurricane Andrew</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hurricane_Iniki" title="Hurricane Iniki">Hurricane Iniki</a> of 1992. The UAV version of the episode has since been discontinued.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Quotes">Quotes</span><span
