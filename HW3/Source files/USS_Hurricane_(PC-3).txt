third <a href="/wiki/Cyclone-class_patrol_ship" title="Cyclone-class patrol ship"><i>Cyclone</i>-class patrol</a> (coastal) ship. Her keel was laid at <a href="/wiki/Bollinger_Shipyards" title="Bollinger Shipyards">Bollinger Shipyards</a> on 20 November 1991, and she was launched on 6 June 1992 and commissioned on 15 October 1993.</p>\n<p>In 2012, <i>Hurricane</i> made port visits to various cities in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lakes" title="Great Lakes">Great Lakes</a> region to commemorate the bicentennial of the <a href="/wiki/War_of_1812" title="War of 1812">War of 1812</a>.</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="References">References</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=USS_Hurricane_(PC-3)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: References">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<ul>\n<li><i>This article includes information collected from the <a href="/wiki/Naval_Vessel_Register" title="Naval Vessel Register">Naval Vessel Register</a>, which, as a U.S. government publication, is in the <a href="/wiki/Public_domain" title="Public domain">public domain</a>.
