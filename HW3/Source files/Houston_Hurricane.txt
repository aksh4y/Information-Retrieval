Their home field was the <a href="/wiki/Astrodome" title="Astrodome">Astrodome</a>. Their colors were orange, white and red.</p>\n<p>The team was the last of six expansion teams granted for the season and had about three months to sign players and sell tickets. Though the coach, <a href="/wiki/Timo_Liekoski" title="Timo Liekoski">Timo Liekoski</a>, who had been an assistant with the <a href="/wiki/Dallas_Tornado" title="Dallas Tornado">Dallas Tornado</a>, was capable, assembling a competitive team in so short a time would be daunting. In any case, the Hurricane placed last in its first season with ten wins of thirty matches (there were no draws in the NASL) and drew a miserable average attendance of 5,806, with only the <a href="/wiki/Chicago_Sting" title="Chicago Sting">Chicago Sting</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/San_Diego_Sockers_(NASL)" title="San Diego Sockers (NASL)">San Diego Sockers</a> drawing less in the 24-team league.</p>\n<p>For 1979, the Hurricane did much better, winning the division with 22 wins in thirty matches, and Liekoski was awarded Coach of the Year honors.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> But the Hurricane could not do as well in the playoffs, losing to the <a href="/wiki/Philadelphia_Fury_(1978%E2%80%9380)" title="Philadelphia Fury (1978\xe2\x80\x9380)">Philadelphia Fury</a> in two straight. Attendance was better at 6,211 a match but still was next to last in the league - the worst being Philadelphia. Considering that <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kyle_Rote,_Jr." title="Kyle Rote, Jr.">Kyle Rote, Jr.</a> had joined the Hurricane that season, it must have been a disappointment. In any case, Rote left the Hurricane on a relief mission to <a href="/wiki/Cambodia" title="Cambodia">Cambodia</a> and later retired.</p>\n<p>The Hurricane didn\'t do as well in what turned out to be their final season, placing second in the division and again losing in the playoffs versus the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Edmonton_Drillers_(NASL)" title="Edmonton Drillers (NASL)">Edmonton Drillers</a>. They won fourteen and lost eighteen in the expanded schedule, and attendance fell to 5,818 a match, with only the <a href="/wiki/Atlanta_Chiefs" title="Atlanta Chiefs">Atlanta Chiefs</a> and, again, Philadelphia being the only teams with worse gates.
