pass</a> located in the <a href="/wiki/Teton_Range" title="Teton Range">Teton Range</a>, <a href="/wiki/Grand_Teton_National_Park" title="Grand Teton National Park">Grand Teton National Park</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/U.S._state" title="U.S. state">U.S. state</a> of <a href="/wiki/Wyoming" title="Wyoming">Wyoming</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-topo_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-topo-2">[2]</a></sup> Situated at approximately 10,338\xc2\xa0ft (3,151\xc2\xa0m) above sea level, the pass can be accessed from the south by way of the <a href="/wiki/Teton_Crest_Trail" title="Teton Crest Trail">Teton Crest Trail</a> or from the north via the <a href="/wiki/South_Fork_Cascade_Canyon_Trail" title="South Fork Cascade Canyon Trail">South Fork Cascade Canyon Trail</a>. From <a href="/wiki/Jenny_Lake" title="Jenny Lake">Jenny Lake</a> the roundtrip hike is 24.8\xc2\xa0mi (39.9\xc2\xa0km) with a 4,500\xc2\xa0ft (1,400\xc2\xa0m) elevation gain. <a href="/wiki/Schoolroom_Glacier" title="Schoolroom Glacier">Schoolroom Glacier</a> is 100\xc2\xa0yd (91\xc2\xa0m) from the pass.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Views of the west slopes of several of the highest Teton Range peaks as well as <a href="/wiki/Alaska_Basin" title="Alaska Basin">Alaska Basin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Battleship_Mountain" title="Battleship Mountain">Battleship Mountain</a> in <a href="/wiki/Caribou-Targhee_National_Forest" title="Caribou-Targhee National Forest">Caribou-Targhee National Forest</a> are available at the pass.</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="References">References</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hurricane_Pass&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: References">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<div class="reflist" style="list-style-type: decimal;">\n<div class="mw-references-wrap">\n<ol class="references">\n<li id="cite_note-gnis-1"><span class="mw-cite-backlink">^ <a href="#cite_ref-gnis_1-0"><sup><i><b>a</b></i></sup></a> <a href="#cite_ref-gnis_1-1"><sup><i><b>b</b></i></sup></a></span> <span class="reference-text"><cite class="citation web"><a class="external text" href="https://geonames.usgs.gov/apex/f?p=gnispq:3:::NO::P3_FID:1600468" rel="nofollow">"Hurricane Pass"</a>. <i><a href="/wiki/Geographic_Names_Information_System" title="Geographic Names Information System">Geographic Names Information System</a></i>. <a href="/wiki/United_States_Geological_Survey" title="United States Geological Survey">United States Geological Survey</a><span class="reference-accessdate">.
