class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hurricane_(Cradle_to_the_Grave)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The song was a collaboration with <a href="/wiki/Trip_hop" title="Trip hop">trip hop</a> artist <a href="/wiki/Tricky_(musician)" title="Tricky (musician)">Tricky</a>, intended for an album <i>Force of Nature</i>, planned to be released in 1998. Due to heavy disagreements between Jones and Tricky, the album was never completed,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and only a scarce <a href="/wiki/White_label" title="White label">white label</a> 12" single featuring two DJ Emily mixes by of the song was released.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Jones later re-recorded the song as the title track of her first studio album release in 19 years, <i><a href="/wiki/Hurricane_(Grace_Jones_album)" title="Hurricane (Grace Jones album)">Hurricane</a></i>, released October 2008.</p>\n<p>"Hurricane" was used at the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Issa_(clothing)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Issa (clothing) (page does not exist)">Issa</a> Spring/Summer 2010 fashion show at <a href="/wiki/London_Fashion_Week" title="London Fashion Week">London Fashion Week</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Naomi_Campbell" title="Naomi Campbell">Naomi Campbell</a> was the opening model to the show who strutted to the line "I\'ll be a hurricane".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Track_listing">Track listing</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hurricane_(Cradle_to_the_Grave)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Track listing">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<ul>\n<li><b>12" single</b></li>\n</ul>\n<dl>\n<dd>A.
