Highly prolific, he began career in the late 1960s as an associate producer and editorial supervisor on <a href="/wiki/The_Andy_Griffith_Show" title="The Andy Griffith Show">The Andy Griffith Show</a>, before going on to direct many episodes of shows like <i><a href="/wiki/The_Six_Million_Dollar_Man" title="The Six Million Dollar Man">The Six Million Dollar Man</a>,</i> <i><a href="/wiki/Ironside_(1967_TV_series)" title="Ironside (1967 TV series)">Ironside</a>,</i> <i><a href="/wiki/Dallas_(1978_TV_series)" title="Dallas (1978 TV series)">Dallas</a>,</i> <i><a
