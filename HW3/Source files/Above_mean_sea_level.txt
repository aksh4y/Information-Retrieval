Mean sea levels are affected by <a href="/wiki/Climate_change" title="Climate change">climate change</a> and other factors and change over time. For this and other reasons, recorded measurements of elevation above sea level might differ from the actual elevation of a given location over sea level at a given moment.</p>\n<p></p>\n<div class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Metres_above_sea_level&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Uses">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>Metres above sea level is the standard measurement of the elevation or altitude of:</p>\n<ul>\n<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Geographic_location" title="Geographic location">Geographic locations</a> such as <a href="/wiki/Town" title="Town">towns</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mountain" title="Mountain">mountains</a> and other <a href="/wiki/Landmark" title="Landmark">landmarks</a>.</li>\n<li>The top of <a href="/wiki/Building" title="Building">buildings</a> and other <a href="/wiki/Nonbuilding_structure" title="Nonbuilding structure">structures</a>.</li>\n<li>Flying objects such as <a href="/wiki/Airplane" title="Airplane">airplanes</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Helicopters" title="Helicopters">helicopters</a>.</li>\n</ul>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="How_it_is_determined">How it is determined</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Metres_above_sea_level&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: How it is determined">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The elevation or altitude in metres above sea level of a location, object, or point can be determined in a number of ways. The most common include:</p>\n<ul>\n<li><a href="/wiki/Global_Positioning_System" title="Global Positioning System">Global Positioning System</a> (GPS), which triangulates a location in reference to multiple <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Artificial_Satellite" title="Artificial Satellite">satellites</a>.</li>\n<li><a href="/wiki/Altimeter" title="Altimeter">Altimeters</a>. They typically measure <a href="/wiki/Atmospheric_pressure" title="Atmospheric pressure">atmospheric pressure</a>, which decreases as altitude increases.</li>\n<li><a href="/wiki/Aerial_photography" title="Aerial photography">Aerial photography</a>.</li>\n<li><a href="/wiki/Surveying" title="Surveying">Surveying</a>.</li>\n</ul>\n<p>Accurate measurement of historical mean sea levels is complex. Land mass subsidence (as occurs naturally in some islands) can give the appearance of rising sea levels. Conversely, markings on land masses that are uplifted due to geological processes can suggest a lowering of mean sea level.</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Other_measurement_systems">Other measurement systems</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Metres_above_sea_level&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Other measurement systems">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Foot_(length)" title="Foot (length)">Feet</a> above sea level is the most common analogue for metres above sea level in the American measurement system.</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Abbreviations">Abbreviations</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Metres_above_sea_level&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Abbreviations">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>Metres above sea level is commonly abbreviated mamsl or MAMSL, based on the abbreviation AMSL for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Above_mean_sea_level" title="Above mean sea level">above mean sea level</a>. Other abbreviations are m.a.s.l.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BC_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-BC-1">[1]</a></sup> and MASL.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TD_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-TD-2">[2]</a></sup></p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="See_also">See also</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Metres_above_sea_level&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: See also">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<ul>\n<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Metres_above_average_terrain" title="Metres above average terrain">Metres above average terrain</a></li>\n<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Metres_above_ground_level" title="Metres above ground level">Metres above ground level</a></li>\n<li><a href="/wiki/List_of_places_on_land_with_elevations_below_sea_level" title="List of places on land with elevations below sea level">List of places on land with elevations below sea level</a></li>\n</ul>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="References">References</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Metres_above_sea_level&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: References">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<div class="reflist" style="list-style-type: decimal;">\n<div class="mw-references-wrap">\n<ol class="references">\n<li id="cite_note-BC-1"><span class="mw-cite-backlink"><b><a href="#cite_ref-BC_1-0">^</a></b></span> <span class="reference-text"><cite class="citation journal">Ricardo Bressani, Ricardo;
