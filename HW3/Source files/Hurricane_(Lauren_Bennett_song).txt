We all at least know someone who has dealt with it or have yourself. This is a story to show from my eyes what it feels like to suffer from this but also the other perspective to lose someone to this."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> It was later announced on <a href="/wiki/Twitter" title="Twitter">Twitter</a> that a portion of the proceeds from the single would be donated to The Campaign to Change Direction,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> an organization dedicated to change the culture of mental health in America.<sup class="reference"
