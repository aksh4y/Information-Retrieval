expedition's first three crew members xe2x80x93 one us astronaut and two russian cosmonauts xe2x80x93 arrived at the station on board soyuz tma-01m on 10 october 2010 expedition 26 officially began the following month on 26 november{2] when half of the crew of the previous mission expedition 25 returned to earth on board soyuz tma-19{2] the rest of the expedition 26 crew xe2x80x93 one us
