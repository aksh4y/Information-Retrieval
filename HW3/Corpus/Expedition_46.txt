mission{5]on 15 january 2016 tim peake and timothy kopra began an eva at 12:55 gmt a failed voltage regulator (1b ssu) was replaced during the eva{6] this eva marked the first official spacewalk by a while chasing tim peake through the space station in a recorded video with the backing track of yakety sax{9]{10] the following day a video message from tim peake was played at the 2016 brit awards
