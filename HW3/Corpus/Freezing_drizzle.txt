near the surface its metar code is fzdz{1]contents1 formation2 effects3 see also4 referencesformation{edit]freezing drizzle is formed in low level stratus type clouds when vertical motion is weak it consists of relatively small drops light in nature freezing drizzle generally occurs when drizzle forms in an airmass at below freezing temperatures but warmer than -10xc2xa0xc2xb0c (14xc2xa0xc2xb0f) at such a temperature the water droplets stay supercooled as there are few ice nuclei to change them to ice crystals (see freezing rain) in winter arctic conditions it can happen at even lower surface temperatures as the air is even cleanereffects{edit]when freezing drizzle accumulates on land it creates an icy layer of glaze freezing drizzle alone does not generally result in significant ice accumulations due to its light low-intensity nature however even thin layers of slick ice deposited on roads as black ice can cause extremely hazardous conditions resulting in vehicle crashessee
